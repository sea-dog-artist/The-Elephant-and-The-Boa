About the author

For most of my life, I was a sailor — navigating oceans and sketching stories inspired by life at sea. Whether it was sharing funny moments with fellow sailors or drawing up practical instructions, storytelling was always part of my journey.

Recently, life gave me a new course: the opportunity to move to the United States. Here, I’ve decided to explore a dream I’ve carried quietly for years — to become a book author.

The story of the Elephant and the Boa is my first published tale, a playful step into this new world of imagination. But it’s only the beginning. I have many more stories to tell, and I can’t wait to share them with you.

- Sea Dog Artist
